🕐 Timestamp Converter Demo

Highlight any timestamp below, right-click, and convert it to your chosen timezone.

How to use

  1. Click the extension icon in the toolbar to set your target timezone
  2. Highlight any timestamp below by clicking and dragging
  3. Right-click the selection
  4. Click "Convert timestamp →" in the context menu
